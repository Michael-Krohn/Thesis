\abstract{
We report on the effects of ionizing radiation on 65 nm CMOS transistors held at approximately $-20 \,^{\circ}$C during irradiation.  The pattern of damage observed after a total dose of 1 Grad is similar to damage reported in room temperature exposures, but we observe less damage than was observed at room temperature.}
